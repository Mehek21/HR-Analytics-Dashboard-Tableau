# Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using **Tableau** to analyze employee demographics, salary distribution, workforce trends, and leave behavior at **Walnut Chocolates**.

The dashboard helps HR teams and business leaders understand workforce composition, compensation trends, employee growth, and leave patterns to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## Business Problem
Organizations need HR insights to understand:

- Workforce composition
- Salary distribution
- Employee growth trends
- Gender representation
- Leave balance patterns
- Education vs salary relationship

This dashboard converts raw HR data into meaningful business insights.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Workforce Distribution
Packaging Associates and Production Operators make up the largest employee groups.

### 2. Gender Representation
Female employees slightly outnumber male employees.

### 3. Workforce Age Pattern
The majority of employees belong to the **30–35 age group**.

### 4. Salary Insights
Employees with **Master’s Degrees earn the highest salaries on average**, showing a relationship between education and compensation.

### 5. Leave Analysis
Leave balances vary significantly across job roles and gender.

---

## Interactive Features
✅ Clicking a job role in the **“Who works with us?”** chart dynamically filters the **Top Salary Holders** table.

For example:
Selecting **Sales Representative** updates the dashboard to display the highest-paid employees in that role.

This enables role-based workforce exploration and improves HR decision-making.
